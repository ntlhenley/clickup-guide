# Account Strategist Guide

## Responsibilities of Account Strategists  
- Act as the main point of contact for clients and internal teams  
- Develop and maintain project timelines  
- Ensure alignment between client objectives and project deliverables  

## What Should Flow Through ClickUp  
- All project tasks and timelines  
- Client communication records and feedback  
- Internal team collaboration notes  

## What Should Not Flow Through ClickUp  
- Personal task lists not related to client work  
- Sensitive information that should remain confidential  

## Task Setup Guidelines  
1. Clearly define the task title and description  
2. Assign appropriate team members  
3. Set realistic due dates and priorities  
4. Use tags and custom fields for easy filtering  

## Best Practices for Managing Production Work  
- Regularly update task statuses to reflect progress  
- Communicate proactively with clients and internal teams  
- Utilize ClickUp's reporting features to track productivity  
- Review completed tasks for continuous improvement.
