# Production Team Guide

## Responsibilities
- Oversee and manage project timelines to ensure smooth delivery of tasks.
- Collaborate with team members and stakeholders to define project scope and objectives.

## Work on Subtasks
- Break down larger tasks into manageable subtasks for efficient tracking.
- Assign subtasks based on team member strengths and workload.

## QC Expectations
- Adhere to quality control protocols to ensure deliverables meet project standards.
- Conduct regular reviews and feedback sessions to maintain high-quality output.

## Communication Guidelines
- Utilize project management tools (e.g., ClickUp) for task updates and progress tracking.
- Maintain open lines of communication with all team members via instant messaging or weekly standups.

## Key Considerations for Working with Strategists
- Understand strategists' objectives and incorporate their feedback early in the process.
- Facilitate brainstorming sessions to align on strategies and creative approaches.
