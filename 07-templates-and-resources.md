# ClickUp Task Templates

## Overview
ClickUp task templates allow you to create standardized tasks that can streamline your workflow and ensure consistency across projects. This guide covers how to use ClickUp task templates, where to find them, time estimates, due dates, and expectations for using these templates effectively.

## How to Use ClickUp Task Templates
1. **Creating a Template:**  
   a. Go to any task within ClickUp.  
   b. Click the three dots at the top right corner.  
   c. Select "Save as Template" from the dropdown menu.

2. **Using a Template:**  
   a. When creating a new task, select "Use Template".  
   b. Choose the desired template from your saved templates list.

3. **Editing a Template:**  
   a. Go to your templates section.  
   b. Click on the template you want to edit.  
   c. Make the necessary changes and save.

## Where to Find Task Templates
- **Built-in Templates:** ClickUp offers a variety of built-in templates that can be accessed from the templates gallery.
- **Your Templates:** Access your saved templates by navigating to the Templates section in your Space or Folder settings.

## Time Estimates
When using templates, it’s important to provide time estimates:
- **Set realistic time frames:** Consider the complexity of the task and past performance.
- **Use time tracking:** Leverage ClickUp’s time tracking feature to compare estimated versus actual time spent on tasks.

## Due Dates
1. **Assign Due Dates:** Ensure that all tasks created from templates have due dates assigned to maintain project timelines.
2. **Use Automation:** Utilize ClickUp’s automation options to automatically set due dates based on task creation.

## Key Expectations for Using Templates
- **Consistency:** All team members should use the same templates to maintain uniformity in task management.
- **Regular Updates:** Update templates regularly to reflect changes in processes or project requirements.
- **Feedback and Improvement:** Encourage team members to provide feedback on templates to enhance their effectiveness.
